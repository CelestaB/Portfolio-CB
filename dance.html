Dance

I grew up in Saint Paul, Minnesota where I trained at Classical Ballet Academy among various other dance schools. For high school, I went to Saint Paul Conservatory for the Performing Artists, and graduated from the University of Minnesota Twin Cities with a BA in dance. Safe to say, I've managed to incorporate dance into all aspects of my education. I have also attended many workshops and summer intensives including Alonzo King Lines Ballet summer intensive, Nutmeg Conservatory's summer intensive, and more.
